  • Asset-Light Operation: Adopt an asset-light operation model, focusing on core algorithm research and development.

  • Customized Services: Provide customized solutions for customers to meet different customer needs.

4. Analysis of Market Gaps

Technical Gap Areas

  • Computational Optics Technology: Although computational optics technology has begun to be applied, there are still large technical gaps in niche areas such as wavefront coding and light field imaging.
  • Multi-Modal Fusion Technology: Technology for deep fusion of multiple modal information such as images, voice, and text is still in the development stage.
  • Quantum Dot Filter Technology: Quantum dot filter technology can significantly improve image color reproduction and brightness, but its current application is still limited.
  • New Sensor Technologies: New sensor technologies such as event-driven sensors and polarization sensors are still in the initial stage of application in smartphone cameras.

Underexplored Market Demand Areas

  • Professional Creation Tools: The high-end photography tool market for professional creators has not been fully developed.
  • Medical and Health Applications: Applications of smartphone cameras in medical and health fields, such as skin diagnosis and health monitoring, have not been fully developed.
  • Industrial Inspection Applications: Applications of smartphone cameras in industrial inspection, quality control, and other fields have also not been fully developed.
  • Educational Applications: There is also significant market potential for applying smartphone cameras in education, such as homework correction and knowledge recognition.

Regional Market Gaps

  • Third- and Fourth-Tier Cities & Rural Markets: Although the penetration rate of smartphones in third- and fourth-tier cities and rural areas is already high, algorithm optimization for the needs of users in these areas is still insufficient.
  • Overseas Emerging Markets: Chinese smartphone manufacturers are increasing their share in overseas emerging markets such as Southeast Asia, South Asia, and Africa, but there is still significant room for localized algorithm optimization for these markets.
  • Specific Industry Markets: Specialized camera algorithm solutions for specific industries such as logistics, retail, and catering have not been fully developed.

5. Market Development Trends and Outlook Forecast

Trends in Market Competitive Landscape

  • Increased Market Concentration: In the next 2-3 years, the concentration of the smartphone camera third-party algorithm solutions market will further increase, and the market share of leading enterprises will further expand.
  • Professional Algorithm Companies Focus on Niche Areas: Facing competition from both chip manufacturers and smartphone manufacturers' in-house algorithm teams, professional algorithm companies will focus more on specific niche areas.
  • Increased Market Share of Chip Manufacturers: With the improvement of chip integration and AI processing capabilities, the competitiveness of chip manufacturers such as Qualcomm and MediaTek in the algorithm field will further increase.
  • Strengthened In-House Algorithm Teams of Smartphone Manufacturers: Smartphone manufacturers such as Huawei, OPPO, and vivo will continue to strengthen their in-house algorithm teams to achieve product differentiation.

Technology Development Trends

  • Deep Integration of AI Technology: AI technology will achieve deeper integration with camera algorithms, becoming the core driver of future market development.
  • Breakthroughs in Computational Optics Technology: Computational optics technology will become an important development direction for future smartphone camera algorithms.
  • Optimization of Multi-Camera Collaboration Technology: Multi-camera collaboration technology will be further optimized to achieve a more natural and efficient shooting experience.
  • Improvement of Video Processing Technology: Video processing technology will become an important development direction for smartphone camera algorithms.

Market Size Forecast

From a global perspective, the size of the computational photography market has grown exponentially in recent years. The market will grow from USD 2.134 billion in 2024 to USD 2.681 billion in 2025, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 10%.
